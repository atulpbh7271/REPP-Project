The Real Estate Price Prediction project serves to provide precise property price estimates in Indian Rupees, leveraging factors such as area, bedrooms, bathrooms, and location. It empowers users with data-driven insights for confident decision-making. This project aims to enhance transparency in property valuations, promote data-driven choices through a user-friendly web interface, and foster a deeper understanding of the Indian real estate market.
